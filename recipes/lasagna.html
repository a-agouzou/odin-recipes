Lasanga

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish that has become a classic comfort food enjoyed worldwide. Its name is thought to have originated from the Greek word "lasanon," referring to a type of flat, thin pot. Traditional lasagna typically consists of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese. The dish is then baked until golden and bubbling. One of the distinctive features of lasagna is its layered composition. The pasta sheets act as a sturdy foundation, separating and supporting the various flavorful components. The meat sauce, often made with ground beef or a combination of meats, is cooked with tomatoes, herbs, and spices, providing a savory and robust flavor

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (about 9-12, depending on the size of your dish)
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces/170g each) tomato paste
  • 2 cups (500ml) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil

Instructions Steps

  1. Cook the Noodles:
    • Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain excess fat
    • Add chopped onions and garlic to the skillet and sauté until onions are softened.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, red wine (if using), oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  3. Cook the Noodles:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with one beaten egg. Mix well.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
    • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top.
    • Spread a portion of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    • Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. Bake:
    • the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  6. Serve:
    • Allow the lasagna to rest for a few minutes before slicing.
    • Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
